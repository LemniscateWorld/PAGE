ABOUT LEMNISCATE AND SAVIOR

SAVIOR is the first stage of the Lemniscate metaverse. We call it “Savior Mission”.

Ice Bear is a planet 700 million light-years away that is full of resources needed by humans in the old world, but the lab at the center of the Ice Bear universe has lost control of animals. Dr. Shumway, chief scientist of Lemniscate on Ice Bear, is trapped in it. Lemniscate Command will send 10,000 elite troops to resolve the Ice Bear lab crisis, explore the new planet, and prepare for the immigration of human beings. Savior is a highly playable game, players accomplish missions, complete daily tasks, and could also dive into fantastic explorations of many wonderful things.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lemniscate?

emniscate is Bernoulli’s curve. It is a function graph that embodies mathematical beauty, i.e., symmetry, harmony, abstraction, simplicity, precision, unity, singularity, and sudden change. It also has valuable artistic beauty. This is exactly what Lemniscate metaverse wants to express. That’s why we name the project after it.

What is Savior?

Savior is the first stage of the Lemniscate metaverse. Lemniscate command headquarters will pick 10,000 elite fighters to save the ICE BEAR from lab crisis, explore the abundant resources, and prepare for human immigration.

SAVIOR is highly playable. Players accomplish missions, complete tasks, and dive into fantastic exploration of everything in the SAVIOR metaverse. Players can see the nature waking up in spring and the silent heavy winter snow, dive into the thousand-mile-deep sea, and land in the sky city to see the whole world. When players discover a new biological species or a world map in the SAVIOR metaverse, they receive exploration rewards in SAV tokens.

How to play Savior?

The first batch of players can enter the game by buying NFT characters (limited to 10,000 at launch). New characters can only be created by players with NFTs. Character NFTs can generate new character NFTs in pairs. Each one can create up to 8 new character NFTs. The higher the generation No., the higher the generation time and the token fee.

When players buy initial NFT characters, they become heroes and saviors in the parallel world. They carry the responsibilities of human immigration and build a truly free country.

What is LMN?

LMN is the governance token in Lemniscate, and it has the following functions: DAO governance: token holders could participate in all on-chain governance proposals.

Ecosystem applications: for different use cases in Lemniscate ecosystem.

What is SAV?

SAV is the game currency in SAVIOR. It can be traded freely on crypto exchanges.

How to produce LMN tokens?

Players who purchase initial character NFTs get LMN tokens based on their transaction volume; in addition, there will be BOSS being placed in the SAVIOR game at regular time for all players to attack, and the LMN tokens will be divided according to the proportion of their contribution. LMN tokens will also be automatically released from private sales, Lemniscate foundation, and ecosystem building.

What are the use scenarios of LMN tokens?

Create new NFT characters: Players who have already had character NFTs could choose to create new character NFTs with certain amount of LMN and SAV when met certain conditions.

Reinforce weapons: Reinforcing weapons need to cost certain amount of LMN and SAV. In addition, purchasing limited chatting background, or emojis also costs some LMN and SAV.

Land development: When players build constructions on the land, they create item NFTs (such as schools, restaurants, hospitals, etc.) and then use some governance token LMN to compound the land NFT and item NFT to finish the building process.

Land transfer fee: Certain amount of LMN is needed when making land deals or transfers.

How to produce SAV tokens?

The only way of producing and gaining SAV is through playing game.

Accomplish missions: accomplish missions set in the plot and gain SAV.

100- story lab challenge: To save Dr. Sheldon and get through the crisis, players will take the 100-floor lab challenge and gain SAV after getting through.

Players get SAV rewards for completing daily tasks.

Lab patrol: Each ecological zone in the lab has a guarding BOSS. After passing the guardian, the patrol function will be activated, and players could collect construction resources and small amount of SAV rewards through patrol.

What are the use scenarios of SAV tokens?

Reinforce weapons: Each weapon NFT could be reinforced for 20 times and reinforcement costs SAV and LMN.

Repair weapons: Weapon NFT all has limited durability. When being used for a long period of time, the durability decreases, and when the durability drops to 0, the weapon will become useless, which could only resume normal use after being repaired. Repairment costs SAV.

Create new character NFTs: Players who have already had character NFTs could choose to create new character NFTs with certain amount of LMN and SAV when met certain conditions.

How to get and develop land in Savior?

Each land NFT is a 900 ft2 plot (actually, a 9,000,000 ft2 land image). Initially, there are 9,000 plots. When a user builds a new NFT (e.g., a school) on this land, it creates an NFT of the new item first and then combines the land NFT and the item NFT for asset creation (this consumes governance tokens). 9,000 plots will be sold as NFTs. Holders of land NFTs can create any NFT of their own on their land.

How to guarantee the uniqueness of Savior characters?

The characters in Savior are NFTs. NFT is used to create verifiable digital scarcity, digital ownership, and the possibility of asset interoperability across multiple platforms. NFT is used for specific applications that require unique digital items such as encrypted games, encrypted art, collectibles, etc. Each character is unique, and each one of them has unique attributes (serial number, character name, attribute value, etc.) and has a unique NFT.

How do I use my character NFT outside of Savior?

The character NFTs in Savior are generated on the most open, decentralized, and secure blockchain. It gives them such traits: digital scarcity, real ownership, authenticity, and most importantly, interoperability.

The character NFTs players own can be used in Savior, and many other games. All Savior data and in-game assets can be viewed by third party (via our API or blockchain explorer), allowing other to build tools and character experience around the character NFTs.

Why do I need a crypto wallet?

Savior is a decentralized game built on the blockchain. It doesn’t require real name verification and doesn’t require any personal info from users. Players only need to have a digital currency wallet to join the game. When setting up your wallet, please make sure you put your private keys or mnemonic phrases in a safe place. If you forget them, you can’t access your assets. Private keys and mnemonic phrases are the only ways to retrieve your in-game assets.

How do I play the game without character NFT?

The first batch of players can only enter the game with character NFTs. Some NFT owners don’t have enough time to participate in the game to gain revenue, or they have extra NFTs but don’t want to sell them, while some players are interested yet cannot participate in the game without the NFTs. To boost the liquidity of NFT within the ecosystem and allow more elite fighters to participate in the game to save the ICE BEAR crisis, Lemniscate has created an innovative lending platform. This platform acts as go-between for the lender (NFT owner) and the borrower (NFT borrower) and providers impartial and smooth services for them through smart contract, which guarantees that the borrower shall never transfer nor destroy the NFT and shall return the NFT to the owner within the agreed timeframe under the lending agreement.
